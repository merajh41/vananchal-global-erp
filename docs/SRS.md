# Vananchal Global ERP

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Planning

---

# Vision

Vananchal Global ERP is a cloud-based business management system designed for traders, wholesalers, distributors, transport businesses, and construction material suppliers.

The goal is to provide one platform for managing inventory, sales, purchases, transport, customers, suppliers, invoices, reports, and business analytics.

The system is modular, scalable, AI-ready, and suitable for businesses of all sizes.

---

# Objectives

- Manage inventory
- Generate invoices
- Manage sales
- Manage purchases
- Manage transport
- Track customer credit
- Export reports to PDF
- Export reports to Excel
- AI-powered business assistant
- Mobile friendly
- Secure cloud access
